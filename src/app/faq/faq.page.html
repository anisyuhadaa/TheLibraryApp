FAQ
  When will the book be updated?

The book in d'library will be updated once a week and an ongoing webtoon will update based on the date in their own release date


  How do I recommend a book?

You have to go to Recomend Us, and enter the tittle of the novel or webtoons and select category/types of your book


  Do I have to buy a coins to read   every chapter on d'Library

NO, you don't need the coins to read the chapter or buy the books to read.
